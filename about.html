Who We Are

Our goal at Empowering the Nation is to promote good change by community involvement, training, and education. We think that information is an incredibly potent instrument that has the capacity to improve communities and change lives. Our programs are created to provide people the abilities and tools they need to prosper in this dynamic world. Empowering the Nation, which was established on the values of inclusion, creativity, and quality, provides a wide selection of workshops and courses that are designed to suit the requirements of students at all phases of their academic and career paths. Our programs, which range from technical expertise to personal growth, are designed to motivate and prepare participants for success. We're dedicated to changing the world by fostering growth and development possibilities. Come together with us as we, one empowered person at a time, strive for a better future for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals in our community through accessible skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. Founded by Precious Radebe, our initiative aims to provide vital opportunities for those who have historically been denied the chance to enhance their education and skill set. By developing a comprehensive web page and mobile app, we will effectively advertise our services, facilitate inquiries from potential customers, and offer competitive quotes for training programs. Our training not only equips participants with marketable skills that increase their employability and earning potential but also fosters entrepreneurship, enabling them to establish their own small businesses. We are committed to meeting the needs of both individuals seeking to upskill and employers looking to enhance their workforce capabilities, ultimately contributing to a more skilled and self-sufficient community.
