6 Month

The six-month courses offered at a fee of R1500 each aim to equip individuals with essential skills in various practical areas. The First Aid course focuses on providing awareness and basic life support, covering topics such as wound management, CPR, and handling respiratory distress. The Sewing course teaches alterations and garment tailoring, including techniques for stitching, threading a sewing machine, and designing new clothing. The Landscaping course provides knowledge on creating and maintaining gardens, emphasizing the use of indigenous and exotic plants, aesthetic design, and the integration of fixed structures. Finally, the Life Skills course helps individuals navigate basic necessities by teaching skills such as opening a bank account, understanding labor laws, and enhancing reading, writing, and numeric literacy. Each course is designed to empower participants with valuable skills for personal and professional development.

6 Week

The six-week courses, offered at a fee of R750 each, focus on essential skills for child and baby care, cooking, and garden maintenance. The Child Minding course provides foundational knowledge in caring for infants and toddlers, covering the specific needs of children from birth to six months, seven months to one year, and toddlers, as well as the importance of educational toys. The Cooking course emphasizes preparing nutritious family meals, teaching participants about nutritional requirements, different types of proteins, carbohydrates, and vegetables, as well as meal planning and cooking techniques. Lastly, the Garden Maintenance course equips individuals with basic gardening skills, including understanding water restrictions, the watering needs of indigenous and exotic plants, pruning and propagation methods, and planting techniques for various plant types. Each course aims to enhance participants' capabilities in these practical areas, promoting personal growth and skill development.
